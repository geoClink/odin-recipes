World Famous Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food, featuring layers of savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It’s a labor of love that rewards you with deep, complex flavors that taste even better the next day.

The key to a great lasagna is the balance between the acidity of the tomato sauce and the richness of the cheese. By slow-simmering the sauce and using high-quality cheeses, you create a dish that is sturdy enough to slice but soft enough to melt in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 12 to 15 Lasagna noodles (oven-ready or boiled)
  • 1 lb Ground beef (80/20 lean)
  • 1/2 lb Italian sausage (mild or spicy)
  • 45 oz Marinara sauce (roughly two jars)
  • 15 oz Whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 Large Egg (to bind the ricotta)
  • 4 cups Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup Fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 small Yellow onion, diced

Steps to Complete the Dish

  1. Brown the ground beef with diced onions and garlic in a large skillet.
  2. Stir in the marinara sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with one egg and chopped herbs.
  4. Spread a thin layer of sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat layers until the dish is full, finishing with a thick layer of mozzarella.
  7. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
